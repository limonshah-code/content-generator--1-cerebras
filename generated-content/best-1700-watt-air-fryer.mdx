## What to Look for Before Buying the Best 1700 Watt Air Fryer

### Find Your Need - Compatibility and use case considerations
Understanding how you plan to use an air fryer shapes your purchase. Large families may need a 10‑plus quart model for batch cooking, while a single‑person household might prefer a compact 5‑quart unit. Consider whether you want a single‑function fryer or an all‑in‑one oven that can bake, roast, dehydrate and even steam. Matching the appliance to your cooking habits ensures you get the right capacity and functionality without waste.

### Budget - Setting realistic price expectations ($X-$Y ranges)
Air fryers span a wide price spectrum. Entry‑level models sit around $70‑$100 and provide basic fry and bake functions. Mid‑range options between $120‑$180 often include multiple presets, larger capacities and accessories. Premium units can exceed $250, offering advanced features like rotisserie, steam infusion and odor‑erase technology.

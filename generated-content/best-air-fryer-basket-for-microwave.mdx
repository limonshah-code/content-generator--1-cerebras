## What to Look for Before Buying the Best Air Fryer Basket For Microwave

### Find Your Need
Assess whether you need a basket that works with a conventional oven, a dedicated microwave air‑fry function, or both. Consider the types of meals you plan to prepare—single‑serve snacks versus full meals—and whether you want a multi‑functional set that includes trays or racks.

### Budget
Set a realistic price range. Basic stainless‑steel baskets typically sit between $15‑$30, while premium multi‑functional sets with copper trays or dual‑zone technology can exceed $150. Align your budget with the features that matter most to you.

### Key Features
Look for perforated mesh or crisper designs that promote 360° airflow, oil‑catching trays, heat‑resistant materials, and dishwasher‑safe construction. If you want to reduce oil usage, a basket with built‑in oil drainage is essential.

### Quality & Durability

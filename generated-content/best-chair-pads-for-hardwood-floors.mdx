## What to Look for Before Buying the Best Chair Pads For Hardwood Floors

### Find Your Need - Compatibility and use case considerations
Understanding the type of furniture you plan to protect is essential. Chair legs come in round, square, or irregular shapes, and pad dimensions must match those profiles. Consider whether you need pads for dining chairs, office chairs, stools, or decorative items. Matching the pad shape and size to the leg ensures a secure fit and optimal floor protection.

### Budget - Setting realistic price expectations ($X-$Y ranges)

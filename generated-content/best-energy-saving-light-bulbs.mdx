## What to Look for Before Buying the Best Energy Saving Light Bulbs

### Find Your Need - Compatibility and use case considerations
Identify the fixture type, base size (E26, E12, etc.) and whether you need dimmable or non‑dimmable bulbs. Consider the room’s purpose: kitchens and workspaces benefit from daylight tones, while bedrooms often prefer warm white for a relaxing atmosphere.

<p>Look for a balanced NPK ratio, preferably around 3‑1‑2 or 6‑2‑4 for fiddle leaf figs. Micronutrients such as iron, magnesium and potassium boost leaf color and root health. Slow‑release technology or foliar delivery can reduce watering frequency.</p>

### Portability/Size
<p>Consider bottle size relative to your storage space. Large‑volume liquid feeds are cost‑effective for many plants, whereas small bottles are handy for spot‑treatments or travel.</p>

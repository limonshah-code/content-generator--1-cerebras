## What to Look for Before Buying the Best Glass Spray Bottle

### Find Your Need - Compatibility and use case considerations
Understanding how you intend to use the bottle is the first step. Whether you need it for household cleaning, plant misting, essential‑oil diffusion, or hair care will dictate the ideal capacity, nozzle type, and material. A bottle meant for heavy‑duty cleaning should handle thicker liquids, while a gardening bottle benefits from a fine mist and a smaller size for easy placement.

### Budget - Setting realistic price expectations ($X-$Y ranges)
Glass spray bottles range from budget‑friendly single units around $10 to premium sets that exceed $30. Determine how much you’re willing to spend on features like silicone sleeves, bamboo caps, or accessory bundles. Remember that a higher upfront cost often translates to longer lifespan and reduced plastic waste.

### Key Features - Most important technical specifications

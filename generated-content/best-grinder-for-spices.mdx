## What to Look for Before Buying the Best Grinder For Spices

### Find Your Need - Compatibility and use case considerations
Determine whether you primarily grind coffee beans, whole spices, herbs or a mix of everything. A grinder dedicated to fine powders may have tighter clearance, while a multi‑functional model needs interchangeable bowls. Consider batch size—if you often prep large meals, a higher capacity will save time.

### Budget - Setting realistic price expectations ($X-$Y ranges)

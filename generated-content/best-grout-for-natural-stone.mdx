## What Is the Best Way to Choose grout for natural stone?
1. **Assess the Surface** – Identify stone type, existing sealant condition and grout colour.  
2. **Define Your Goal** – Decide whether you need protection, stain removal, colour enhancement or mold control.  
3. **Check Compatibility** – Ensure the product is safe for your specific stone and grout.  
4. **Evaluate Performance Claims** – Look for verified protection periods, stain resistance and pH neutrality.

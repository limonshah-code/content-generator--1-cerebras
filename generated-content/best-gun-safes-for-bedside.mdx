## What to Look for Before Buying the Best Gun Safes For Bedside

### Find Your Need

Consider which firearms you intend to store, the typical environment (home nightstand, travel, car), and whether you need extra space for valuables such as jewelry or documents. A bedside safe should accommodate the length and grip size of your most common handgun while still fitting the available surface area.

### Budget

Prices range from under $50 for basic mechanical safes to over $300 for premium biometric models with added tech. Set a realistic range—$70‑$120 often delivers solid security and features, while $200‑$300 unlocks advanced fingerprint scanners, wireless charging and larger capacities.

### Key Features

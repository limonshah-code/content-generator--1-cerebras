## What to Look for Before Buying the Best High Pressure Nozzle For Garden Hose

### Find Your Need - Compatibility and use case considerations

Identify the tasks you will tackle most often—watering delicate seedlings, blasting away mud, or giving pets a quick rinse. Match the nozzle’s pressure range to those needs and ensure it fits a standard 3/4‑inch garden hose, which is the most common thread size in the United States. If you plan to attach the nozzle to a pressure‑washer or a pump, verify that the connections are compatible.
